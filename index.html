Biography

I am Gener Aviles, a DrSc student at Universidad Autónoma de Baja California under the mentoring of Juan Iván Nieto Hipólito PhD. Before starting gradute studies I got a degree as a medical doctor from Universidad de Montemorelos and worked as such for six years in the areas of community medicine, family practice, health administration and public health. I was trusted the responsability as health director for the county of General Terán in the State of Nuevo León México for it´s 2013-2016 administration.

After finishing my responsibilities as a public servant I decided to pursue graduate studies in a program that would allow me to explore data analytics to facilitate medical benefits to patients. I was allowed to pursue this at the computer science school in Universidad Autónoma de Baja California, under the mentoring of María de los Ángeles Cosío León, DrSc, for the Masters in Science.

I am currently focused on the application of Computational Topology tools in medical data, specifically images, to obtain information relevant in the clinical practice and, eventually, caregivers and patients. The broader scope of what I do can well be described as Computational Medicine. I am also interested in data analytics and machine learning with applications in the medical domain.
